Fighting Eviction and Homelessness in NYC

Rising rents and lack of affordable housing options swept NYC residents into its highest homeless rate since the Great Depression of the 1930s. Tenant Counsel Network aggressively protects New York City renters from this record breaking crisis, with legal representation ranking as the most effective safeguard against eviction.

Many struggling tenants believe that they are too behind on rent to reasonably dispute their eviction. This fatal misconception couldn’t be further from the truth. While landlords win most NYC eviction cases, *it is only because they are far more likely than tenants to hire attorneys*.

Expertly Navigating Tenant Laws to Protect Your Home

Because NYC housing court leans heavily in favor of renters, a strong lawyer can preserve your home in the most unseeming circumstances. Ways we can leverage pro-tenant laws in your favor include arguing that your eviction is retaliatory (and therefore illegal), that your landlord is violating proper court procedures, discriminating against you, or overlooking necessary repairs.
